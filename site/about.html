上海彩志纸业有限公司成立于2001年8月，是专业经营各种进口、国产高、中档色纸、花纹纸、特种纸、艺术纸、充皮纸、包装纸及装帧材料为主的企业。

十几年来，我们追随着世界纸张的潮流，把各国纸张提供给各界的用户；同时，与国内工厂配合着不断开发生产出价廉物美，适应各种档次的纸张品种。

另外，我们密切的关注市场的设计、印刷、包装行业的发展，从提供一张纸张的打样，到产品成品的服务；从普通的在校学生到专业人员，我们都以齐全的产品、合理的价格、充足的库存，鼎力相助，并赢得了众多客户的信任和支持。

目前，我们的销售网络，全国500多家紧密合作的分销合作商。这为我们向客户提供迅速完美的服务奠定了基础。

Shanghai CaiZhi Paper Co., Ltd. was established in August 2001, a professional business specializing in imported and domestic large and medium-sized color paper, various patterns paper, special paper, art paper leatherette paper, wrapping paper, and binding materials.

For more than a decade, we have followed the global paper trend, supplying paper from various countries to consumers from multiple industries; simultaneously, we have partnered with domestic factories to grow and produce low-cost, high-quality paper, adapting to multiple grades of paper varieties.

Furthermore, we pay careful attention to the design,printing, and packaging industry. From providing a piece of paper to finished products, from ordinary school students to professionals, we will use all complete products, fair prices, ample inventory, and do our best to help you, and have earned the confidence and support of many customers.

So far, our sales network in China has over 500 affiliated distribution partners working together closely. It lays the foundation for our customers to receive effective and reliable service.

Our career goal is to be energetic and continually innovate. I hope our paper will assist you in achieving a perfect elaboration of your product!
